Logs and kindling

Our hardwood logs (predominantly Ash and Beech) are suitable for both open fires and wood burners. They give excellent slow burning warmth. We also supply large nets of kindling.

Ready to burn

All the wood we sell is independently certified as having a moisture content of 20% or less. This means it is ready to burn without needing further seaasoning, reducing the amount of smoke and harmful pollution caused by higher moisture levels.
